ANNIVERSARY

Corporate Event Management Services in Bhubaneswar, Odisha

Just On Events is a team of professional coordinators, who pay particular attention to the organization of anniversary parties, dedicated to love and memories. We guide you through the whole process to create a wonderful event based on the theme that you both find perfect. No matter whether the atmosphere of your celebration is intimate or grand, we offer a range of services to ensure your special day is nothing short of perfect.

Event Planning and Coordination

Our professional planners have the commitment to make your anniversary plans come to reality. We take full responsibility of all consultations and planning up to the point of the actual event. Consultation service includes the choice of the location, the creation of the theme, the schedule, and selection of the theme, so the event would be organized up to the smallest detail. Our team will take care of all the arrangements and you can sit back and watch your anniversary event turn out well.

Decoration

Choosing the right venue is the key to making a memorable anniversary celebration. Just On Events guides you to choose the right venue for your tastes. Our skilled decorators transform and enhance the venue with personalized touches, from floral arrangements to custom lighting, creating an atmosphere that reflects your unique love story.

Catering and Culinary Experience

No anniversary celebration is complete without perfect food and beverages. When it comes to catering services Just On Events are the best caterers in town to ensure that your guests are served some mouth-watering meal which will suite their tastes. No matter whether you want a formal fine dining menu or just a party buffet with mouth-watering sweet endings, we have solutions to you in accord with the choice of your taste. We also offer custom beverage stations that tell your story.

Entertainment and Music

Whenever celebrating an anniversary, one needs to select appropriate music and entertainment boosting an event’s mood. When it comes to the music our staff works closely with the most popular DJs or live bands in order to provide with the right music for the evening. Just On Events will not only provide a DJ that will play your favourite love songs, but create an atmosphere of aura for you.

Photography and Videography

Do not let any beautiful moment go unnoticed during your anniversary celebration and book with us photography and videography services. All our photographers and videographers know how to capture the key moments, as well as candid interactions that make your event unique. From fabulous celebration photos whether to stunning portraits we delve into the evening of celebration to provide you nothing but the best.

On-Site Management

Just on event management offers on-site management services during the anniversary celebration of the couple. Our team is responsible for managing all of your requirements and, ensuring that all events occur at the proper time, and handling all of your guests for the day. Leave it to us, sit back and watch as our team of professional take care of your anniversary event right from the word go.
