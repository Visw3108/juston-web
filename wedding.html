WEDDING

Corporate Event Management Services in Bhubaneswar, Odisha

Just On Events is all about designing and planning amazing Indian weddings that are both traditional yet stylish. As we offer solutions starting from the selection of venue, decor and catering services, to hiring of entertainment troupes, and overall coordination of the events. Our experienced team ensures that your wedding is flawlessly executed, reflecting your unique style while honouring cultural traditions. Let us bring your dream wedding to life with precision and passion, so you can focus on celebrating your love.

Haldi Ceremony

The Haldi ceremony is one of the happiest rituals before a wedding and Just On Events guarantees that it will be celebrated with happy colors. We bring together family and friends for a fun-filled and colorful event and make sure that the turmeric paste on the bride and the groom gets the happiness and vibe of the celebartion. With our exquisite arrangement skills to display beautiful furnishings, personalized decor and arrange a welcoming and joyous ambiance in order to transform this ritual into a beautifully memorable experience.

Mehendi Celebration

Mehendi the art of love and we are privileged to be a part of this ceremony on behalf of our beauty. From the ornate henna designs to the fairytale like lighting, theme, and everything in-between, Just On Events pays attention to each aspect. Be it a intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we ensure to have loads of music, dance and delectable food to make the Mehendi glow on the bride’s hands and feet.

Sangeet Ceremony

The Sangeet is a night of music, dance and enjoyment and at Just On Events we assist you in making the evening a grand one. At Just On Events, we make your dream come true by delivering stylish and coordinated entertainment. From traditional songs to modern beats, we ensure the Sangeet is a high-energy event, full of laughter, joy, and unforgettable memories. We make sure everyone takes to the dance floor, dancing the night away to remember the special moments that kick-off the wedding celebrations.

Barat Procession

The Barat is one of the biggest highlights of the Wedding Day and we ensure that it turns into a great show. Starting from where the groom and his side enters to the Barat, to following the ceremonial sequence of events. Just On Events organizes this special occasion in the best manner that can be imaged. We take care of transportation, decorations and coordination by including music and dance to the joyful processions so that the bride’s side is prepared to joyously welcome the groom.

Reception Party

Reception is the last official event in your wedding celebrations and it’s a time to celebrate love and new beginnings with family and friends. We at Just On Events organize beautiful concepts in decor, lighting, and amazing and distinct details of your taste. Making all your moments magical and coordinated from decoration to food making it possible for you to enjoy the night with your family and friends. We have a wide range of services for all type of receptions, small intimate dinners and grand celebrations, and we pay attention to every small detail to ensure that your reception meets and exceeds your needs.
